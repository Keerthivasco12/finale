Mahindra XE60

About

Mahindra XE60 is a compact SUV from Mahindra.It is available in 3 variants: XE60, XE60 Plus, and XE60 Plus Plus.It is available in 2 engine options: 1.5L Diesel and 1.5L Petrol.

Specifications of Mahindra XE60

  • Range : 400 - 500 km
  • Top Speed : 160 km/h
  • Charging Time : 100% in 1.5 hours
  • Battery Capacity : 60.2 kWh
